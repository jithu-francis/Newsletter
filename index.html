IEEE LaunchPad Kerala 2024

Introduction

IEEE LaunchPad Kerala is a premier event organized by the IEEE Kerala Section in collaboration with GTech MuLearn. This event aims to connect engineering students and graduates with leading companies in the technical and engineering sectors. By fostering these connections, LaunchPad Kerala strives to drive progress and innovation within Kerala's job market.
